Perinet is an industrial IoT company based in Cottbus. Its AI platform connects
LLM workflows to real-time MQTT sensor streams. The platform exposes FastAPI REST
APIs and is deployed with Docker and Kubernetes.

PeriChat is a chatbot that runs on the Perinet platform. MessageSense performs
real-time anomaly detection over the MQTT message stream. Both PeriChat and
MessageSense are containerized and benchmarked before release.

LangGraph orchestrates the multi-agent workflows. RAGAs and MLflow are used to
evaluate retrieval quality. The MQTT broker feeds sensor data into MessageSense,
which raises alerts when anomalies are detected.
